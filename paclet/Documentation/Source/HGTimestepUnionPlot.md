## Usage

`HGTimestepUnionPlot[evolutionResult, step]` draws the union of all states at `step`, with vertices coloured by local dimension.

## Details

- `evolutionResult` is the output of `HGEvolve`.
- See `Options[HGTimestepUnionPlot]` for the analysis and styling options.
