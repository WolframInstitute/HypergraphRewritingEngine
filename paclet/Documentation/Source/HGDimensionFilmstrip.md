## Usage

`HGDimensionFilmstrip[evolutionResult]` shows a grid of timestep union graphs coloured by dimension, one per step.

## Details

- A filmstrip view of how dimension evolves.
- See `Options[HGDimensionFilmstrip]` for the analysis and styling options.
